How to Play

Simon is a game to test your memory. Once the start button is pushed the computer will light up one random square. It is your turn to click the same square, then click check streak to see how your choices measure up. If you choose correctly click hit me and the computer will continue to add one more random square to the sequence. Play goes back and forth until your sequence is checked and you made a wrong move. Game over. Play again.
